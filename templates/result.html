Some tips to prevent Heart Disease

Eat Heart-healthy Diet

Eat a variety of healthy foods, including fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.Healthy fats like olive oil and avocado. Eat Fatty fish rich in omega-3s (salmon, mackerel).Avoid high-cholesterol foods and increase fiber intake.

Manage Stress

Chronic stress increases heart disease risk.Practice relaxation techniques like meditation, deep breathing, or yoga.

Exercise Regularly

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise (like brisk walking or cycling) five days a week.Try to get atleast 7 hours of sleep each night.

Quit smoking & Limit Alcohol

Smoking damages blood vessels, raises blood pressure, and increases the risk of heart attacks.Moderate alcohol consumption can have heart benefits, but excessive drinking raises blood pressure.

Control Blood Pressure

High blood pressure strains the heart.Monitor your levels regularly and follow a low-sodium, balanced diet to keep it in check.
